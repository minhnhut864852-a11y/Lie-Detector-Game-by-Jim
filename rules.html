📖 How to Play

The host will randomly pick 3 players as speakers across 3 rounds — one speaker per round, no repeats!

Each speaker gets 90 seconds to prepare 3 statements — 2 of them are false and 1 of them is true. Say them out loud with a straight face and try to fool everyone!

The rest of the players have 60 seconds to guess which statement is the true one by typing their answer in the MS Teams chat.

🏅 Correct guesser earns +1 point for each correct guess.

🎭 Speaker earns +1 point for every 3 wrong guesses — the better you lie, the more you score!

After 3 rounds, the player with the highest score wins. Good luck! 🏆
